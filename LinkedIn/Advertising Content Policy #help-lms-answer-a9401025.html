Cryptocurrency and Related Products Advertising

Last updated: 8 months ago

These guidelines are provided to you to help you comply with LinkedIn’s Advertising Policies when advertising cryptocurrency-related products or services. Advertisers are responsible for understanding and complying with all applicable laws, regulations, and industry codes pertaining to their ads.

LinkedIn restricts ads that promote the purchase, sale, holding, storage, management, borrowing, lending, or exchange of cryptocurrencies, as outlined below.

Cryptocurrency Exchanges, Investment Products, and Wallets

Ads must meet the following requirements:

  • Cryptocurrency exchange, investment product, and wallet advertisers must have prior authorization to serve ads on LinkedIn. If you're not an authorized advertiser, reach out to your LMS representative for details.
  • Ads promoting cryptocurrency exchanges, investment products, and wallets must come from authorized advertisers.
  • Ads must target within the United States, United Kingdom, Switzerland, Germany, France, Netherlands and United Arab Emirates.
  • Ads must target finance-related professionals. See the finance-related professional targeting section below to learn more. Ads promoting cryptocurrency exchanges, investment products, and wallets may not be targeted at consumers or a general audience. 
  • Ads must not target members under 18 years of age.
  • Ads must not promote products on our prohibited list.

Finance-related professional targeting

LinkedIn is a professional network where members come to connect, learn, and share news related to their profession. For cryptocurrency ad types described above, ads must be targeted to finance-related professionals and must be relevant to their work.

Finance-related professionals are generally defined as any person (or entity) associated with the financial or cryptocurrency industry or the provision of financial-related services, advocacy, research, or policy development related to finance.

All ads must comply with LinkedIn’s Advertising Policies, applicable terms, and all applicable laws, regulations, and industry codes. We reserve the right to reject, approve, or remove any ad for any reason at our sole discretion.

Prohibited products and services

Ads promoting the following cryptocurrency-related products are prohibited:

  • Ads that are misleading or make unrealistic claims, such as guaranteed returns on investment.
  • Ads promoting the sale, purchase, or free offering of specific cryptocurrency coins or tokens.
  • Ads for initial public offerings of digital tokens or new cryptocurrency tokens.
  • Ads for decentralized autonomous organizations (DAO).
  • Ads for decentralized finance applications and services (DeFi).
  • Ads for cryptocurrency mining services.
  • Ads for cryptocurrency staking services.
  • Ads for non-fungible tokens (NFT).
  • Ads promoting free gifts or sweepstakes offering cryptocurrency tokens.

Consumer cryptocurrency & related products ads

We're beginning to introduce consumer ads promoting cryptocurrency products and wallets in the United States for a select group of authorized financial customers, and we'll evaluate the experience for members and customers in the months ahead to determine if we'll offer this advertising option more broadly.
