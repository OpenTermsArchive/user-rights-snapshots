AI Disclosures
UPDATED 19 November 2025
These AI Disclosures supplement Meta's AI Terms of Service.
Creating images or videos of yourself using Meta AI, uses artificial intelligence ("AI") technology, developed by Meta, that allows you to generate and share imagined photos or videos of yourself in, for example, different contexts and settings. To work, each time that an image or video is generated, the AI technology transforms images or voice recordings (where available) of yourself that you provide into an embedding representing your visual or voice characteristics and uses the embedding to generate images or videos that may look or sound similar to you ("Generated Media"). This technology is not facial or voice recognition. The embedding, which cannot be used to identify you, will be deleted within just a few seconds of its creation.
When you create images or videos of yourself with Meta AI, Meta may use the images, videos and voice recordings (where available) that you provide and other information (for example, your prompt instruction) in accordance with Meta's Privacy Policy, including to (a) evaluate how well the AI technology performed or (b) perform other business obligations, such as fulfilling any compliance obligations. When you share the Generated Media with other users, your prompt may also be shared with those users.
By creating images or videos of yourself with Meta AI, you represent, warrant and agree that the images and voice recordings that you provide are of you and do not include or otherwise incorporate any other person(s). You further expressly waive and release any claim that might be brought by you or on your behalf against Meta or any Meta affiliate alleging that these AI Disclosures or the conduct described herein fail to comply with or otherwise violate in any respect the Illinois Biometric Information Privacy Act, 740 ILCS 14, or the Texas Capture or Use of Biometric Identifier Act, Tex. Bus. & Com. Code § 503. If any aspect of this Paragraph is deemed invalid or unenforceable, that aspect shall be severed and not affect the validity or enforceability of any other aspect.
By creating images or videos of yourself with Meta AI, you agree to the use of AI technology, and to the collection and use of your information as described above. You also understand that Meta AI may not accurately generate headwear or facial accessories captured during onboarding. This may result in such items being partially or fully omitted in images generated by Meta AI by yourself or other users. You can always choose not to use these features and such information will not be collected and used. Similarly, you can disable the feature at any time by deleting the photos of yourself and the voice recording that you provided. You can do so by going to Meta AI settings in your chat with Meta AI on Instagram, WhatsApp or Messenger, or by going to the settings in the Meta AI app.
