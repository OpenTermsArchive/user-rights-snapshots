TikTok Creator Fund Participation Agreement

This TikTok Creator Fund Participation Agreement (the “Agreement”) is entered into by and between the below named creator (“Creator”) and TikTok Information Technologies UK Limited, a company registered in England with company number  10165711 and its registered address at Wework, 125 Kingsway, London, England WC2B 6NH. (“TikTok”) as of the signature date below (“Effective Date”) with respect to Creator’s continued activity on the TikTok platform (“Platform”) and participation in the Creator Fund Program (“Program”), each a “Party” and together the “Parties”.

This Agreement incorporates and is supplemental to the EU Terms of Service of the TikTok Platform (found at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/terms-of-use?lang=en#terms-eea), and the TikTok Community Guidelines (found at https://www.tiktok.com/community-guidelines?lang=en), each as may be amended from time to time. The EU Privacy Policy of the TikTok Platform (found at https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy?lang=en#privacy-eea)  will continue to apply to your use of the Platform (together with the EU Terms of Service and the TikTok Community Guidelines, the “Policies”). In the event of a conflict between this Agreement and the Policies, this Agreement will prevail during the Term (defined below), solely with respect to Creator’s participation in the Program. Upon termination of this Agreement, the Policies will remain in effect and continue to apply to Creator’s use of the Platform.

The Parties hereby agree to the following: 

1. Eligibility 

At all times during participation in the Program, Creator agrees to meet all eligibility requirements for the Program, as follows: 

A. Creator is 18 years of age or older as of the Effective Date;

B. Creator’s TikTok account on the Platform is in good standing and remains in compliance with the Policies;

C. Creator has at least 10,000 authentic followers; 

D. Creator has at least 100,000 authentic video views in total over the last 30 days prior to the Effective Date; and

E. Creator has a PayPal account which is linked to bank account (such Paypal account being the “Nominated Account”), or such other payment method as required by TikTok from time to time,

the ("Eligibility Criteria").

2. Term 

The Term of this Agreement shall begin on the Effective Date and last until terminated as set forth below.

3. Fees 

Provided that the Creator at all times during the Term meets the Eligibility Criteria TikTok will pay Creator the Fees net of any third-party transaction fees (and subject to the Minimum Payment Threshold defined in the clause below).

By participating in the Program you agree that there's no maximum nor minimum amount of Fees (defined below) that the Creator could be paid.

Fees are calculated:

  •  dynamically and based on the factors mentioned below; and
  •  as a portion of the total EU TikTok Creator Fund.

The "Fees" are the sums payable as determined by TikTok based on a number of factors that influence how Fees are calculated for videos under the Program. This includes the region of the view, number of video views, video engagement, authenticity of a view, as well as adhering to the Policies.

Creator’s legitimate, unique video views on qualifying videos are determined by TikTok, and exclude video views that TikTok determines: (i) are artificially produced or obtained; (ii) are the Creator’s own views; or (iii) are otherwise obtained in violation of the Policies. The Fees may be impacted by the country of the views origin.

The way the Fees are calculated is subject to change in accordance with the provisions of this Agreement.

The Fees shall be the only sums payable to the Creators for the participation in the Program. No additional payments are due to the Creator for  use of the Content in accordance with the Policies.

4. Payment Terms 

Where a Fee is due to the Creator in respect of a post in accordance with this Agreement, TikTok will provide Creator with access to data showing how much it has earned during a thirty (30) days period. Creator acknowledges there may be a delay between the post appearing on the Platform and reporting to the Creator on the sums due.

Where the Fees due to the Creator are above the minimum payment threshold of £10.00 (pounds)  ("Minimum Payment Threshold") TikTok shall pay such Fees into the Creator's Nominated Account within thirty calendar (30) days of the end of the month in which the invoice was received.  

Creator is responsible for all taxes (if any) associated with the Program. All payments to Creator from TikTok in relation to the Program shall be inclusive of tax (if applicable).

In the event Creator is a taxable person for VAT purposes in Creator’s country of residence TikTok will issue a self-billing VAT invoice to Creator at the time that payment is due. Ahead of Payment of the Fees, Creator shall sign the Self Billing Agreement through the Tax Declaration process, to comply with self-billing requirements.  Creator is solely responsible for completing and signing the Self-Billing agreement.

Payment of the Fees into the Nominated Account shall satisfy TikTok’s obligation to pay the Fees under this Agreement. Creator is responsible for withdrawing sums from the Nominated Account.

In the event that the sums due to the Creator at the end of  any calendar month are less than the Minimum Payment Threshold, such sums shall become payable once the sums payable exceed the Minimum Payment Threshold.

5. Termination

TikTok may terminate this Agreement immediately upon notification to the Creator:

a. if Creator materially breaches the terms of this Agreement and/or the Policies;

b. if Creator becomes involved in any situation or occurrence which reflects unfavourably upon Creator or TikTok (including the Platform or the Program); or

c. if Creator makes any derogatory public statement or comment concerning TikTok (including the Platform or the Program).

The statutory right of extraordinary termination for good cause remains unaffected.

Creator may terminate this Agreement at any time on written notice to be sent via email.

TikTok may terminate this Agreement on thirty (30) days’ written notice to be sent via email. The TikTok Terms of Service and Privacy Policy  shall survive termination of this Agreement.

Any and all notices permitted or required to be given under this Agreement shall be sent to the email address set out below.

-    Notice for Creator shall be the Creator’s email as indicated below. Creator shall keep TikTok informed of any changes to its email address. In the event Creator’s email is no longer operational, notice to it shall still be valid unless Creator previously informed TikTok in writing (including by email)  of a change in the Notice email address.

-    Notice to TikTok is feedback@tiktok.com

Following termination, TikTok shall have no further payment obligations hereunder save in relation to Fees that accrued prior to the effective date of termination.

6. Representations and Warranties 

Creator represents, warrants and undertake that:

a. Creator is over the age of 18 and does not fraudulently conceal his/her age;

b. Creator agrees to comply with the terms of the Policies;

c. no payment to any third-party shall be due by TikTok for the activities described in this Agreement, including but not limited to the Creator’s participation in the Program;

d. all content created by Creator will be Creator’s own original creation, it will be made in accordance with the terms of this Agreement and the terms of the Policies and do not and will not infringe, misappropriate or otherwise violate the Intellectual Property or other rights of any third party;

e. it has informed TikTok whether it is registered for VAT in its country of residence;

f.  Creator shall not solicit or employ the use of any method, software, automated script, technology which attempts to artificially increase views or interactions with Creator’s User Content (as defined in the TIkTok Terms of Service), including for purposes of inflating the Fees and Payment Terms hereunder; and

g. it shall not falsely state, misrepresent, or conceal affiliation with any person or entity.
